    How your CAS RO Purifier works.


  • Particulate Filter:

    The water passes through the particulars filter which removes fine and coarse and rust particles of impurities from water.


  • Pre Activated Carbon Filter:

    It adsorbs chlorine and organic impurities like harmful pesticides from water. It also adsorbs bad taste and odor-causing organic compounds from water.


  • Sediment Filter:

    The RO removes bacteria, virus, protozoa, parasites and reduces dissolved salts, hardness, pesticides, It also reduces heavy metals like Fluoride, Cadmium & Mercury.


  • Intensity UV Rays Disinfection/Silver Impregnated Post Carbon Cartridge:

    The water is simultaneously passed through UV process from taste enhancer and deactivates cirus and Bacteria present in water. The water from RO membrane is also passed through UV for double purification/Silver nano technology prevents bacteria growth and gives you pure and tasty water.
